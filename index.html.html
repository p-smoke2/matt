About Mere Afrique

Mere Afrique is inspiring everyone around the world to rediscover Africa via adventure.

Mere Afrique is the first ever of its kind. Africa happens to be the new found wealth of the world. at Mere Afrique we are about to unfold what africa really is, and the never seen parts of africa, be prepared for the next vital stage in africa's history. At Mere Afrique there are no boundaries, reasoning cut across everyone and everyone inspires everyone.

The Adventure(Africa by Road)

This adventure as the name implies ,is the drive overland round Africa.This adventure is to be streamed life/broadcasted.A team shall be selected for this adventure. The drive is expected to be between 33 to 36 Countries, depending which Countries poses threat to the team at the time of adventure,we expect that the Teams adventure takes between 8/9 weeks. The greatest and most historical places in Africa should expect visits during this adventure, places like the great pyramid of Egypt, Shara desert, the great Namib desert in Namibia believed by scientists to be the world oldest desert estimated to be around 50 million years old. Mount Kilimanjaro which the team proposes to climb to the highest point. The Kigali Genocide Memorial, Rwanda, Robben Island, South Africa and such likes. You also stand a chance to find out yourselves the reasons Ethiopia and Liberia could not be colonized. Theres a lot to learn,discover,research,demonstrate and invent during this adventure.
Everyone on this adventurous team has independence and capability to propose activities to the team during travel,this adventure we expect to beam light on countless and notable achievements and potential achievements by group and individuals in Africa.Register to stand a chance to participate

Please Note: This adventure is opened to everyone around the world for selection without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national or ethnic origin, disability, profession or other factors irrelevant to participate. But you have to be atleast 18 years old to participate

Why should I register ?

When you register and pay the token for registration, you stand the chance to be selected as one of the participants to dare the drive round Africa, you would not have to pay for this trip all expenses paid.
